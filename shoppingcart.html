Yasmine Shopping Cart

Description: This is a Laravel-based full-stack e-commerce application featuring boutique-style hijabs and accessories. Customers can browse curated products, add items to their cart, and checkout using a clean, responsive interface. Admins can add/edit products and manage the store from a secure dashboard.

Technologies: Laravel (PHP), Blade, MVC, HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL

Team Member: Yasmine Chiboub (Full project – frontend, backend, design, database)
